Faizur Rahman Ideal Institute

Faizur Rahman Ideal Institute is a prominent educational institution located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Established in 1998 by Md. Faizur Rahman, the institute was founded with the mission of combining modern education with religious values to nurture students into responsible and ethical citizens.

The institute operates across three campuses in Malibagh, Banasree, and Basila Road. It emphasizes a balanced approach to education, integrating academic excellence with moral and cultural development. Over the years, it has gained a reputation for producing commendable results in public examinations and fostering a disciplined learning environment.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a transformative learning experience that empowers our students to become lifelong learners and leaders in their communities. We are committed to providing a nurturing and inclusive environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, creativity, and ethical responsibility. Our programs are designed to meet the needs of a diverse student body, and our faculty and staff are dedicated to delivering rigorous academic instruction, practical skills training, and experiential learning opportunities that prepare our graduates to succeed in their chosen fields. We strive to be a catalyst for positive social change by promoting research, innovation, and community engagement. Our ultimate goal is to empower our students to make a meaningful difference in the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a premier educational institution that is recognized globally for excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. We strive to create a learning community that fosters intellectual curiosity, creativity, and diversity. Our graduates will be known for their leadership, ethical responsibility, and contribution to the betterment of society. We aim to provide a transformative educational experience that prepares our students to face the challenges of a rapidly changing world and to be lifelong learners. We envision our institution as a hub of innovation and knowledge creation, where our faculty, staff, and students work together to make a positive impact on the world. Our ultimate goal is to be a driving force for positive social change, both locally and globally.

Headmaster's Speech

There is no alternative to education for the development of the nation. A nation can rise to the pinnacle of development through education. The nation must be educated to develop its own civilization and culture. However, one cannot help but be dismayed by the horrors of the current degradation of social and human values. It is becoming more and more clear day by day that education devoid of religious and moral values is incapable of developing the true human qualities of man. Therefore, nation-building with religious and moral values is the need of the hour.

This sacred responsibility of nation-building mainly rests on the educational institutions of the country. Inspired by this belief and understanding, we have been trying to reflect the ideals of religious and moral values in the students’ thinking, following and serving since the inception of our school.
